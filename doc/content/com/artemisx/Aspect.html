class com.artemisx.Aspect

Used by Systems to match against groups of entities. This may be conceptually difficult to understand, so read Ari's comments about it here:

http://gamadu.com/artemisx/manual.html#Aspect http://gamadu.com/artemisx/javadoc/com/artemis/Aspect.html
